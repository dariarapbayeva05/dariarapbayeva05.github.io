Did you know that Shymkent is the largest city of KZ by its size?

Interesting fact!

The southern megapolis occupies

the first place in Kazakhstan in terms of the size of its territory of

1,170 square kilometers. The city area of Shymkent is almost equal

to the territory of Moscow.

Alsoo...

Shymkent is the sunniest city in the country. In Shymkent, the number of sunny days reaches 300 per year. Our winter is moderately warm, there are thaws up to +10 °C and cold spells up to -15 ° C. And the summer in Shymkent is the hottest and longest in the country. The temperature reaches +45 ° C, and in some parts of the region – up to +49 ° C.
